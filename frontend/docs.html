Using Templates

Templates are pre-designed LaTeX structures. They define the layout, fonts, and styling so you can focus on your content.

How to select a template

  1. Navigate to the Templates page from the top menu.
  2. Use the filters (ATS, Minimal, etc.) to find a style that fits your industry.
  3. Click "Use Template" on your preferred design.

What happens next?

After clicking "Use Template," you'll be taken to the Editor page with the template's code already loaded. You can then start replacing the placeholder text with your own experience.

Compiling PDFs

In the world of LaTeX, "Compiling" is the process of turning your code into a visual document.

When to recompile?

Recompile whenever you want to see how your changes look. We use **Skeleton Loaders** during compilation so you can stay in your flow without big overlays blocking your screen.

Compilation Cache

Thanks to our Compilation Cache, if you make a small change and then revert it, the PDF will be served instantly (sub-second) without hitting the server!

Error Handling & Debugging

LaTeX can be picky about syntax. We've built a **Premium Error Panel** to help you solve issues instantly:

Visual Debugging

On failure, the editor automatically highlights error lines in red and adds red status dots in the gutter for instant identification.

Line Detection

The compiler extracts exact line numbers (e.g., l.42) from the logs and smooth-scrolls you to the relevant code block.

Detailed Logs

The Error Panel distinguishes between critical errors (Red) and warnings (Yellow), giving you the full picture of your document's health.

FAQ

Do I need to know LaTeX?

Not at all. You can simply edit the text within the templates. If you are comfortable with code, you can do much more, but it is not required.

Can I download without compiling?

No, because the PDF needs to be "built" from your latest changes first. Always compile one last time before downloading.

Are templates ATS-friendly?

Yes! We specifically tag templates that are optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems to ensure your resume gets through.

Is my data saved?

If you are logged in, your progress is automatically synced to the cloud. If not, your work remains in your browser until you close the tab.
